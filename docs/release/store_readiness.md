# Release Readiness

## App Store Checklist
- Bundle identifier and signing configured.
- Privacy policy URL publicly accessible.
- Terms of service URL publicly accessible.
- In-app disclaimer visible on onboarding and analysis screens.
- Camera/photo permissions usage descriptions localized.
- App metadata localized: EN/ES/DE/FR/RU.
- Final screenshots exported for all required device sizes.
- Build archived and validated in Transporter.

## Google Play Checklist
- Keystore and Play App Signing configured.
- Data safety form completed and verified.
- Permission declarations for camera/media reviewed.
- Localized listings for EN/ES/DE/FR/RU.
- Content rating questionnaire completed.
- Internal testing track smoke-tested before production.

## Legal and Policy Requirements
- Nutrition approximation disclaimer visible before first meal save.
- Explicit non-medical-device statement in settings/legal.
- Account deletion/contact method available from policy links.

## In-app Disclosure Copy
- "Nutrition estimates are approximate and may be inaccurate."
- "Meal recognition can be incorrect. Please review and edit before saving."
- "This app is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice."
